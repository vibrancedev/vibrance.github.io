Pastor Joel & Jane Kim

Joel Kim is a dedicated pastor and a graduate of Rutgers College (2005) and Princeton Theological Seminary (2009), where he earned his Masters of Divinity.


He honed his skills at the International House of Prayer in Kansas City and the Bethel School of Worship in Redding, California. With 17 years of full-time ministry experience, he's served as a youth pastor, worship pastor, and media pastor and is a creative at heart.


Joel is happily married to his wonderful wife, Jane, and they dote on their cherished canine companion, Brie, whom they consider a part of the family.


An enthusiastic sports enthusiast, Joel passionately supports the Brooklyn Nets and the NY Football Giants, along with being a devoted fan of the Korean National Team and its football players.


His heart beats to witness people thrive and find freedom through the boundless love and grace of our Lord Jesus Christ in the Gospel. Joel is equally zealous about empowering creatives to unleash their full potential, igniting the world with their talents for the Gospel's sake.
