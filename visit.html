1. What can you expect at Sunday worship?

Our worship begins with a time of praise, followed by a sermon and ministry time. Every first sunday of the month we celebrate communion together. After worship we have lunch prepared at church so you won’t go home hungry and can fellowship over the breaking of bread!


2. Is there childcare provided?

We are in the process of developing our children’s ministry at Vibrance Church. Currently, children’s ministry is available upon request, and we have dedicated and experienced staff ready to organize a children’s program as needed. If you have children and are planning to visit, we ask that you would kindly DM us at our instagram account (@vibrancechurch) so we can prepare in advance for your child(ren)!
